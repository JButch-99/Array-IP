How does this work?

Welcome to Mr. Roboger's Funhouse! This is a place where you can see him turn your numbers into words! To explain it, here is the fun and helpful guide!

  • 1 = Beep!
  • 2 = Boop!
  • 3 = "Won't you be my neighbor?"
  • It's a descending list of importance. 3 takes priority, and 1 takes least priority!

Numbers are sorted by if they contain one of the above magic numbers! For example, 12 becomes "Boop!", whereas 10 becomes "Beep". It will not change numbers whose number doesn't contain one of our three magic numbers!
